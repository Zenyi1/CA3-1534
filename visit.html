Visit Hermosillo, Sonora

Hermosillo is a beautiful city located in the Sonoran Desert of Mexico. Here are some experiences you shouldn't miss:

  • Exploring the majestic Cerro de la Campana
  • Visiting the historic Palacio de Gobierno
  • Indulging in authentic Sonoran cuisine
  • Strolling through the picturesque Plaza Zaragoza
Cerro de la Campana

Enjoy breathtaking views of Hermosillo from the top of Cerro de la Campana.

Palacio de Gobierno

Discover the rich history of Hermosillo at the Palacio de Gobierno.

Plaza Zaragoza

Take a leisurely walk and soak in the charm of Plaza Zaragoza.
